Frequently Asked Questions

How do I roll the dice?
Simply tap on the dice area or shake your device to roll the dice. You can select the number of dice (1-6) using the + and - buttons.
How do I change the number of dice?
Use the + and - buttons in the "Dice Count" section to increase or decrease the number of dice from 1 to 6.
How do I turn off the sound?
Tap the sound icon in the top-left corner of the app to toggle sound on or off.
How do I remove ads?
Go to Settings and tap on "Remove Ads" to purchase the ad-free version of the app.
The app is not working properly. What should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, make sure you have the latest version installed. You can also try restarting your device.
